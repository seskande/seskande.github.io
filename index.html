Game Instructions
  • Welcome to the Bridgers!
  • You are now a citizen of Dauphine Island. Each week you will be presented with an issue that is challenging the island community.
  • You will receive different information on the issue through the phone and newspaper on your home page.
  • The calendar on the upper right corner will indicate the day of the week. At the end of the week you will be invited to a community gathering with other islanders.
  • During the community gatherings, you will be asked to collectively make a decision in response to the current issue, using the information that you have gathered. Your decisions will impact the needs of you and the island community at large.
  • Make sure to critically examine the information that you are offered and discuss with your fellow citizens to sustain life on Dauphine Island as long and as well as possible.
  • Good Luck!
